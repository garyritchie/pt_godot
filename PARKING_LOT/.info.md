## PARKING\_LOT/

A temporary location to drop inspirational and informative material that may become an asset or `REFERENCE` later.

Files from various sources and types, such as previous projects, stock photos, models, or a random image search. Create an additional folder to identify the source. Locate licensed material to `<asset type>/.../LICENSED` (and document). Move files with no clear license to `REFERENCE/` -- and *use as reference only.*

Folder not version-controlled or part of a backup. Deleted at the end of the project.
